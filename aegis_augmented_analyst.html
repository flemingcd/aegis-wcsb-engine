[ SECTION_01 // THE SPATIAL REALITY ]

Correlating Fuel Age with Subsurface Assets

In the Western Canadian Sedimentary Basin (WCSB), energy producers and underwriters are wrestling with a growing spatial blind spot: Fire-Free Interval (FFI) delta. Areas that have avoided wildfire for over 50 years accumulate extreme timber fuel loads, exponentially increasing catastrophic crown fire risk when ignition strikes.

Rather than relying on static PDF reports or expensive enterprise GIS packages, we engineered Aegis—a zero-cost, interactive web utility that pairs regional wildfire telemetry with asset vulnerability modeling.
