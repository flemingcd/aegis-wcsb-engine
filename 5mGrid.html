// QUICK START TUTORIAL Step 1 of 4

01 // Interactive Map Legend

Use the floating Map Filters Legend in the bottom-right of your screen.

You can click directly on the labels (e.g. active rigs, tenders, or pipeline networks) inside the legend to dynamically toggle their spatial overlays on and off.

02 // Telemetry Popups

Click directly on any map item—including the active drilling rig derrick symbols, regional Crown parcels, or major pipeline routes.

This instantly opens full metadata popups featuring technical parameters, formation targets, and operational status logs.

03 // Contractor Contact Info

When exploring active rigs inside popups, you will find highlighted Operator and Contractor names.

Click on either company name to trigger a smooth in-popup slide-out card displaying mock regional phone numbers, support emails, and physical corporate mailing details.

04 // Land Tenders & Liabilities

Use the top navigation panel tabs to toggle between Rigs and Land Sales.

This view introduces full 16-division Alberta DLS LSD subdivision grids alongside clustered hazard warning markers detailing over 180 regional decommissioned orphan well sites.
